Instructions

  1. First, Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the device
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, and there should be a red dot on the screen
  4. Now press play and stay 3-4 feet away from the device
  5. The Red Paddle is now your paddle, and it will move with your right wrist.
